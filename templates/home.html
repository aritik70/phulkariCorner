The Art of Phulkari

Phulkari, which literally means 'flower work', is a unique style of embroidery traditional to Punjab. It is characterized by the use of darn stitch on the wrong side of coarse cotton cloth with colored silken thread.

At Phulkari Corner, we are dedicated to preserving this beautiful traditional art form by creating authentic handcrafted items that showcase the intricate patterns and vibrant colors of Phulkari embroidery.
